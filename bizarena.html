Game handbook

Everything you need to run the 6-round business simulation across the next 6 sessions.

Philosophy: Each round = 1 quarter of business operation. Teams make decisions about Price, Quantity, Marketing. The system computes actual revenue based on demand function Q = a - bP × (1 + marketing_factor).

6-Round Progression (across 6 sessions)

Round Workflow (~15 minutes)

  1. 0-2 min: Instructor opens app, starts the round, projects onto screen
  2. 2-7 min: Teams discuss — CFO + CEO + CMO decide Price, Q, Marketing
  3. 7-9 min: Each team's CFO walks to instructor desk; instructor enters numbers
  4. 9-10 min: Instructor clicks "Random Event" if round 3 or 5
  5. 10-12 min: Instructor clicks "Finalize Round" — system computes results
  6. 12-15 min: Display scoreboard + history — teams see their position

Computation Formulas

Demand: Q_market = (3000 − 50 × P̄) × N × event_multiplier  (P̄: giá trung bình ngành, N: số đội tham gia)
Attractiveness: A = (P̄ / P)^1.5 × (1 + min(marketing × 0.05, 0.5))
Demand_i: Q_market × A_i / ΣA → Q_sold = min(Q_planned, Demand_i) + tái phân bổ cầu dư
Revenue: DT = P × Q_sold
Cost: TC = FC(15M) × event + 12k × Q_planned + Marketing  (biến phí theo sản lượng sản xuất — rủi ro tồn kho)
Profit: LN = DT − TC
MoS: (Q_sold − Q*) / Q_sold × 100%

Data Persistence

⚠ Important: This app runs LOCALLY in your browser. Data is auto-saved to localStorage. Closing the tab is fine. However, changing computers means losing data — use "Export JSON" at the end of each session for backup.
